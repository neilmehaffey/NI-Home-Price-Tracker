About the NI Tracker

This web application is an advanced interactive tool showing historical data based on Land & Property Services (LPS) and Northern Ireland Statistics and Research Agency (NISRA) residential datasets.

Core Methodology & Indicators:

  • Average vs Median: The median values estimate midpoint distribution, removing skewed transactions (extremely cheap or premium houses).
  • Standardised HPI: Re-indexes the entire trajectory using Q1 2016 as a base value of 100.0 to track standardized momentum independent of raw prices.
  • QoQ & YoY: Calculations represent the dynamic change relative to the preceding quarter or the same quarter in the previous calendar year.
