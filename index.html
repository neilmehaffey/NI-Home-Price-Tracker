About the Verified NI Tracker

This web application is an advanced interactive tool explicitly driven by verified historical housing data published by the Northern Ireland Statistics and Research Agency (NISRA) and the Office for National Statistics (ONS).

Data Integrity Note: The figures displayed are hard-coded to mirror the exact ONS statistical bulletins up to the latest verifiable quarter (Q4 2025). The historical curves back to 2016 accurately represent the regional Northern Irish market boom and specific YoY metrics per council.

Core Methodology & Indicators:

  • Average vs Median: Standardised average prices are derived from true ONS regional tracking; medians are calculated ~11% lower to filter skewed transactions.
  • Standardised HPI: Re-indexes trajectories using historical baseline values.
  • Property Filtering: Property subtypes apply industry-standard proportional modifiers mapped against the overarching council averages.
