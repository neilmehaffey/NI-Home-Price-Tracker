About the NI Tracker

This web application is an advanced interactive tool showing 10 years of historical data based on Land & Property Services (LPS) and Northern Ireland Statistics and Research Agency (NISRA) residential datasets.

Core Methodology:

  • Average vs Median: The median values estimate midpoint distribution, removing skewed transactions (extremely cheap or premium houses).
  • Standardised Index: Index values are base-weighted against Q1 2016 levels for statistical clarity.
  • Inflation Engine: Real prices adjust nominal costs against the UK Consumer Price Index (CPIH) tracker.

Designed for public utility and interactive reference. Open-source under MIT standard licensing models.
